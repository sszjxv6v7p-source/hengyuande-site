我们的 OEM/ODM 服务

Our OEM / ODM Services

  1. 咨询与需求确认：客户提供包装、克重、数量与目标市场。
  2. Inquiry & Requirement: Customer provides packing, weight, qty and target market.
  3. 样品开发：样品时间通常 7–14 天（视工艺而定）。
  4. Sample Development: Typical 7–14 days depending on process.
  5. 批量生产：签订合同并支付定金后安排生产与质量检验。
  6. Mass Production: Production arranged after contract and deposit, with QC.
  7. 包装与出口：可按客户要求的出口规范包装并协调物流。
  8. Packing & Export: Export-ready packing and logistics coordination available.
